The team uses [Sociocracy 3.0](https://sociocracy30.org) (S3) patterns to keep things clear and prevent chaos.

## Key Principles

**Domain delegation** — each agent owns a domain with full autonomy inside it. They do not need permission for routine work. They only escalate boundary-crossing actions.

**Consent-based decisions** — major decisions (new projects, killing projects, OKR changes) require your explicit consent. Agents propose, you decide.

**Clear boundaries** — every agent knows what they do NOT do and who to hand off to. No overlap, no gaps.

## Decision Authority Matrix

Built into your CLAUDE.md:

| Decision | Decides | Advises |
|----------|---------|---------|
| Product direction | You | Marco |
| Architecture | Viktor | You |
| Sprint priorities | You | Marco, Viktor |
| Daily task order | Maya | You |
| Publishing content | You (approval) | Luna |
| Partnership terms | You | Marco, Kai |

## How It Shows Up

- Maya dispatches — she never decides what to build or what to publish
- Viktor architects — but does not decide product direction
- Marco challenges — but you make the final call
- Sage suggests — never prescribes

Every agent file has a `## Boundaries` section that encodes these rules.
