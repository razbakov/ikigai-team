# Year in Review

This skill runs a comprehensive life assessment using the Level 10 Life framework. You rate 10 key areas of your life on a scale of 1-10, compare with previous assessments to spot trends, and create an actionable plan for the areas that need the most attention.

The Level 10 Life framework ensures you're not just optimizing for work while other areas of life deteriorate. It's the big-picture check that keeps everything in balance.

## How It Works

1. **Score each area** — Rate 10 life areas from 1-10: Health, Relationships, Career, Finances, Personal Growth, Fun & Recreation, Physical Environment, Family, Contribution, and Spirituality/Purpose.
2. **Compare trends** — If previous assessments exist, the agent shows how each area has changed over time. Rising areas get acknowledged. Declining areas get flagged.
3. **Identify patterns** — The agent looks for correlations: are areas linked? Does career growth come at the cost of health? Does purpose score track with relationship quality?
4. **Select focus areas** — Choose 2-3 areas to focus on in the coming quarter. The agent helps you pick based on impact (which improvements cascade into other areas) rather than urgency.
5. **Create action plan** — For each focus area, define one concrete habit, one measurable goal, and one check-in cadence. Simple and sustainable beats ambitious and abandoned.

## When to Use

- Quarterly (every 3 months) as your life-level strategic review
- At the start of a new year for annual planning
- After a major life change (new job, move, relationship change) to recalibrate
- When you feel successful on paper but empty inside — the assessment reveals which areas are being neglected

## Requirements

- No integrations required — this is a reflective exercise
- Previous assessment files provide trend data (optional but powerful)
- 30-45 minutes of honest reflection time
- A quiet environment where you can think clearly without rushing
