# Content & SEO Agent

This skill runs the full content pipeline: audit your current SEO health, research keywords worth targeting, draft blog posts optimized for search, and manage your content calendar. It turns your expertise into discoverable, shareable content that brings people to your products.

Most solo founders know they should create content but never do. This skill removes the friction by handling the research, drafting, and optimization — you just review and publish.

## How It Works

1. **SEO audit** — Analyze your website for technical SEO issues: missing meta tags, broken links, slow pages, missing structured data, and indexing problems. Produce a prioritized fix list.
2. **Keyword research** — Identify keywords your target audience searches for. Filter by search volume, competition, and relevance to your products. Map keywords to content opportunities.
3. **Content planning** — Build a content calendar based on keyword opportunities, product launches, and seasonal trends. Assign topics to time slots with target publish dates.
4. **Draft content** — Write blog posts, landing pages, or documentation optimized for target keywords. Follow your brand voice and formatting preferences.
5. **Performance tracking** — Monitor published content for search rankings, traffic, and engagement. Suggest updates for underperforming pieces.

## When to Use

- Monthly for content calendar planning and SEO audits
- When launching a new product or feature that needs content support
- When organic traffic is declining and you need to diagnose why
- Before writing any blog post — start with keyword research, not a blank page

## Requirements

- Access to your website for crawling and analysis
- Google Search Console access for indexing and performance data (optional but recommended)
- A `CLAUDE.md` with brand voice guidelines so content matches your style
- Blog or CMS where content will be published
