# Daily Review

The Daily Review is the most important skill in your agent system. It runs every morning to gather data from all your sources, process your inbox, and create a prioritized plan for the day. Without it, tasks pile up and priorities drift.

This skill pulls from browser history, AI session transcripts, messages, calendar events, and project status to build a complete picture of where things stand. It then proposes a structured daily plan that respects your energy, deadlines, and strategic goals.

## How It Works

1. **Gather data** — The agent collects browser history, AI transcripts, new messages, calendar events, and task board updates from the past 24 hours.
2. **Process inbox** — Any unprocessed messages are triaged through GTD: classify as action, reference, content idea, or trash, then route to the appropriate project.
3. **Check deadlines** — Scan for overdue tasks, approaching deadlines, and blocked items that need your input.
4. **Build the daily plan** — Propose top 3 priorities, list dispatched agent tasks, and map time blocks against calendar commitments.
5. **Deliver the briefing** — Send the review summary via your preferred channel (terminal, Telegram, or Notion).

## When to Use

- Every morning as your first work activity
- After returning from time off to catch up on what happened
- Before important meetings to get a quick status snapshot
- When you feel overwhelmed and need to see everything in one place

## Requirements

- **Notion** — for task board integration and card management
- **Calendar access** — to check today's schedule and deadlines
- A configured `CLAUDE.md` with your review preferences and data source paths
