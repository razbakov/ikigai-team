# Personal Coaching Session

This skill provides a structured coaching conversation that helps you reflect, unblock, and reconnect with your purpose. It's not therapy — it's a thinking partnership where the agent asks questions, holds space, and gently challenges you when needed.

The agent uses assessment data, check-in history, and journaling context to make the conversation relevant to where you actually are, not where you think you should be. The Socratic method ensures insights come from you, not from the agent.

## How It Works

1. **Check in** — The agent asks how you're feeling and what's on your mind. No agenda required — start wherever you are. The agent reads your energy level, not just your words.
2. **Explore** — Through open-ended questions, the agent helps you dig into what's really going on beneath the surface. "What's making this hard?" matters more than "What's the task?"
3. **Reflect** — The agent mirrors back what it hears, helping you see patterns you might miss. It connects today's feelings to previous check-ins and assessments.
4. **Unblock** — If you're stuck, the agent helps identify the root cause: fear, perfectionism, unclear priorities, burnout, or misalignment with values. Different causes need different responses.
5. **Close with intention** — End with one clear intention for the day or week. Not a task list — a mindset or commitment that aligns with what emerged in the conversation.

## When to Use

- When you feel stuck or unmotivated and can't pinpoint why
- During your morning routine as a reflective warm-up
- After a difficult day or week to process what happened
- When you're about to make a big decision and want a thinking partner
- Regularly (weekly or biweekly) to maintain self-awareness and prevent burnout

## Requirements

- No integrations required — this is a conversational skill
- Previous assessments or journal entries help the agent provide continuity (optional)
- A few minutes of honest, uninterrupted time
- Willingness to sit with discomfort rather than rushing to solutions
